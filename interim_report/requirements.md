Interim Project Report Outline

This report is a brief description (roughly 5-10) pages that describes you progress in developing you class project. The goal is to augment the information provided in your midterm presentations to give a clearer pictures of where you are given what you proposed to do in your project proposal as, perhaps, mediate by our discussions.

Due Date: COB Lecture 11, 2025
Deposit report in your File Exchange for your group.

Outline:

Title Page: Document Title(“Interim Project Report”), Author Name, Project Title, University Name, Course number and title, Date.

Introduction: Expand upon the Introduction in your proposal to give a clear picture of the problem.

Technical Approach: How will you solve the problem (now that you have had time to think about it). 
What has changed from your project proposal?
What technologies are you using to develop a solution?
What data sets are you using

Software Architecture: Present one or more diagrams of the software components you are using and how they are integrated into a problem solution.
Briefly describe each component and any effort required to integrate it with the other components.
Discuss how data will flow in your program to generate a solution.

What data set(s) will you use to solve the problem. Briefly describe these data sets, any preparation to make them usable.

Describe what coding and testing you have done to date. This will be continued through the rest of the course, but you should show progress here on completing the project.

Describe any revisions to your deliverables based on progress to date.

Risks: Describe any risks that impact the completion of your project.

Assessment of your status: How do you think you are doing?

Format: Times New Roman, 11, 1-1/2 spacing.
Figures captioned below the figure.
